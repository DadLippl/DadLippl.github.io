Welcome to Vancouver DeMolay

DeMolay is a youth leadership organization for young men aged 12 to 21, built on the values of honor, service, and brotherhood. Through mentorship, ritual, and teamwork, members grow into confident, capable leaders — supported by dedicated adult volunteers and a time-tested program that builds character and community.

This website is here to help families and prospective members learn more about our local Vancouver Chapter. Whether you're curious about what DeMolay offers, looking for upcoming activities, or ready to get involved, we’re here to help. We invite you to explore, learn more about our program, and contact us with any questions.
